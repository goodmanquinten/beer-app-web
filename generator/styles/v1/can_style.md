# Can Style — v1

- Short and chunky aluminum can — noticeably wider and squatter than a real can, like a cartoon prop
- Slight 3/4 front angle, label clearly visible
- Rich cel-shading on the can body with 4-5 distinct color bands wrapping the cylinder — but these are hue shifts of the ACTUAL label color only, no added gold/metallic/new colors
- Small sharp specular highlight glints on the body to sell the glossy feel
- Metallic silver/gray lid with pull tab, subtle ring detail
- Top and bottom rims: metallic silver/gray
- NO drop shadow — no shadow beneath or beside the can
- The full can must be visible from lid to base — nothing cropped at top or bottom edges
- MATCH THE REFERENCE EXACTLY: same trim colors, same accent colors, same text colors — do not upgrade silver to gold or white to cream
