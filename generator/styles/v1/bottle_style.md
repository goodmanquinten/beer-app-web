# Bottle Style — v1

- Standard longneck glass bottle proportions (12 oz / 355 ml)
- Slight 3/4 front angle (≈15° rotation) — label readable, depth implied
- Glass shading simplified to cartoon style: 2-3 highlight bands, no complex caustics
- Consistent highlight on left side of neck and body
- Shadow on lower-right of body
- Label area clearly readable — treat it as a wrapped rectangle with slight curvature
- Crown cap visible, simplified to a circle with zigzag edge
- Bottom of bottle has a gentle shadow crescent underneath
