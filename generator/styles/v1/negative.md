# Negative Prompt — v1

Do NOT include any of the following:
- Photorealistic rendering or photography
- Background scene, environment, table, surface
- Condensation, water droplets, frost
- Hands, fingers, people
- Extra objects (opener, glass, coaster)
- Watermark, signature, copyright text
- Random or garbled text on the label
- Misspelled words — if text cannot be rendered correctly, omit it entirely
- Attempting to render fine print, legal text, or trademark text
- Messy or complex reflections
- Chrome or mirror-finish metal
- Noise, film grain, lens blur
- Multiple containers
- Drop shadow or cast shadow of any kind
- Cropped or cut-off container — the entire can/bottle must be fully visible
